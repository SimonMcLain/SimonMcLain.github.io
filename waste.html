Plastic Waste Generation

How does wealth relate to plastic waste generation?

There is a clear correlation between economic development, measured as GDP per capita, and plastic waste generation.

People in 'first-world' countries generate more kilograms of plastic per person than less developed countries.

Population size does seem a contributing factor.
